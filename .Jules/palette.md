## 2025-05-06 - Dynamic business details and out-of-service status
**Learning:** Enhancing amenity displays with descriptive text and specific icons (e.g., "Out of service" in orange) significantly improves accessibility and user trust, especially for parents who need to know if a changing table is actually functional.
**Action:** Always check for 'out_of_service' states in amenity models and provide clear visual and textual feedback beyond simple boolean states.
