## 2025-05-15 - Improving Bottom Sheet Clarity and Accessibility
**Learning:** Amenities in a bottom sheet should explicitly reflect their state (like 'out of service') both visually and textually to avoid user confusion. Accessibility handles (drag handles) and icon-only buttons must have descriptive content descriptions for screen readers.
**Action:** Always provide explicit visual and textual feedback for non-binary states (yes/no/out-of-service) and ensure all interactive or structural UI elements have proper ARIA-equivalent labels in Android.
